About

A little bit of history ....

Procol Harum ( pronunced /ˈproʊkəl ˈhɑːrəm/) are an English rock band formed in 1967. They are best-known for their recording of the 1967 hit single "A Whiter Shade of Pale " being one of a few singles to have sold over 10 million copies. In 2018, the bandwas honoured by the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame when "A Whiter Shade of Pale " was inducted into the brand-new Singles category. Although noted for their baroque and classical influence, Procol Harum's music also embraces the blues, R&B, and soul.
