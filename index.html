A little bit of history ....

Procol Harum ( pronunced /ˈproʊkəl ˈhɑːrəm/) are an English rock band formed in 1967. They are best-known for their recording of the 1967 hit single "A Whiter Shade of Pale", one of a few singles to have sold over 10 million copies..
